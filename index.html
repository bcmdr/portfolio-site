About

Hello there! My name is Brett Commandeur and I’m a Designer/Developer from Edmonton, AB.

I enjoy creating applications for the web using the latest in HTML, CSS, and Javascript, while making use of frameworks and platforms such as React, Vue, Express, Firebase, and Netlify.

I’m currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science at the University of Alberta after transferring from Film Production at York University in Toronto, ON.

From the big screen to the ones we hold in our hands, my mission has been to enrich the lives of my audience through passion, intrigue and attention to detail.

Projects

GoDo

This productivity app lets you list out all the tasks that need your attention and start the ones you'd like to focus on.

It times how long the task takes and invites you to focus on it again if needed.

It is built with Vue and uses Local Storage to save tasks to your browser.

Live Demo: godo.netlify.com

Orchard

This wellness app lets you list daily goals you'd like to accomplish and mark them as 'done' as you complete them.

It tracks how many days you've completed each goal, resetting their statuses each day.

It is built with React, uses Local Storage to save goals to your browser, and uses the Dragula library for drag and drop reordering.

Live Demo: orchard-react.netlify.com

PopCorn

This entertainment app lets you track your movies by marking them as 'interested', 'seen', or 'favourite'.

It searches The Movie Database to showcase the title, year and poster of movies you may like to track.

It is built with React, and uses Session Storage to cache movie data. It also uses Firebase for Google login capabilities and Netlify functions to hide my API credentials.

Live Demo: popcorn-movies.netlify.com
