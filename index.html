About

Hello there! My name is Brett Commandeur and I’m a Designer/Developer from Edmonton, AB.

I enjoy creating applications for the web using the latest in HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

I’m currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science at the University of Alberta after transferring from Film Production at York University in Toronto, ON.

From the big screen to the ones we hold in our hands, my mission has been to enrich the lives of my audience through passion and intrigue.

Projects

GoDo

This productivity app lets you list out all the tasks that need your attention and start the ones you'd like to focus on.

It times how long the task takes and invites you to focus on it again if needed.

It is built with Vue and uses Local Storage to save tasks to your browser.

Live Demo: godo.netlify.com

Orchard

This wellness app lets you list daily goals you'd like to accomplish and mark them as 'done' as you complete them.

It tracks how many days you've completed each goal, resetting their statuses each day.

It is built with React, uses Local Storage to save tasks to your browser, and allows for drag and drop reordering of your goals.

Live Demo: orchard-react.netlify.com
